Legend Position

Legend Position

Position of the legend. Can be one of the following: 'right' - To the right of the chart. Incompatible with the vAxes option. 'top' - Above the chart. 'bottom' - Below the chart. 'in' - Inside the chart, by the top left corner. 'none' - No legend is displayed.

Chart Alignment

Chart Alignment

Alignment of the legend. Can be one of the following: 'start' - Aligned to the start of the area allocated for the legend. 'center' - Centered in the area allocated for the legend. 'end' - Aligned to the end of the area allocated for the legend.

Start, center, and end are relative to the style -- vertical or horizontal -- of the legend. For example, in a 'right' legend, 'start' and 'end' are at the top and bottom, respectively; for a 'top' legend, 'start' and 'end' would be at the left and right of the area, respectively.

The default value depends on the legend's position. For 'bottom' legends, the default is 'center'; other legends default to 'start'.
